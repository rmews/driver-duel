What are the fantasy challenges?

Each week you play, you will have a series of challenges to pick the correct driver in six different categories. You will have four drivers to choose from and if you pick the driver that outperforms the other drivers, then you picked the correct driver. The challenges include:

  • Which driver scores more stage 1 points.
  • Which driver scores more stage 2 points.
  • Which driver scores more stage 3 points.
  • Which driver finished better.
  • Which driver leads the most laps.
  • Which driver has the highest point differential.

What are streaks?

Streaks are earned by making consecutive correct picks for a category. Streaks also have point multipliers. For example, if you pick the correct driver for who scores the most stage 1 points and do so the next week, you will move up the streak ladder. Prizes and point multipliers are earned at streak levels 4, 6 and 10.


What are the prizes?
  • Correctly pick 4 out of 6 challenges in a week and win $10.
  • Correctly pick 5 out of 6 challenges in a week and win $25.
  • Correctly pick 6 out of 6 challenges in a week and win $100.
  • Make 4 consecutive picks in a category and win $10.
  • Make 6 consecutive picks in a category and win $50.
  • Make 10 consecutive picks in a category and win $100.
