Collect Drivers

Instead of spending hours trying to find the right line-up, like traditional fantasy games, Driver Duel gives each player a choice - pick between two random drivers. This method goes back to the card collecting days of opening packs and seeing if your driver can out-duel the other driver. If you don't like your driver selection, don't worry, you can always redraw one more time.

Random Matchmaking

What good is a game if you're playing alone? Instead of battling against a bunch of other players that have a ton of lineups, your are randomly paired against another player. This other player also receives two random drivers making the driver duel that much more interesting.

Leaderboard

Driver Duel is different than traditional fantasy games when it comes to scoring. Yes, you will earn points each week for laps lead, fastest laps, position differential, and finishing position. You'll also earn points for winning your duel, but instead of those points resetting each race, you'll get to keep those points and track your progress on the overall leaderboard. There are multiple ways to win, so view the FAQ for a complete breakdown.
